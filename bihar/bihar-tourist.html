Top Tourist Places in Bihar

Bodh Gaya

One of the most important Buddhist pilgrimage sites, where Lord Buddha attained enlightenment under the Bodhi tree.

Nalanda

Home to the ancient Nalanda University, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and center of learning in ancient India.

Rajgir

An ancient city with spiritual significance in Buddhism and Jainism, known for its hot springs and scenic hills.

Patna

The capital city of Bihar with a rich history, home to Golghar, Patna Museum, and the iconic Ganga Ghats.

Vaishali

Historic city known as the birthplace of Mahavira and the place where Buddha gave his last sermon.
